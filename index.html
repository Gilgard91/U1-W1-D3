Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

FEATURED IN:

Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin oilve oil, plus more for the pan

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape ring off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries
